ABOUT US

BookHub is passionate about books and encourages reading for both pleasure and education. Everything we do at The Book Hub Publishing Group is in service of this purpose, and working with authors provides us with a powerful tool to make change happen locally and globally. We provide a broad and diverse range of books and to make them available to the widest possible base with a personal flavor.

We urge you to please support local business and local books, and to encourage everyone to keep reading!
